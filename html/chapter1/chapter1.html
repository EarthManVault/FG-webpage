Simple logic and scientific research in cognitive psychology upholds main statements made by her). There are a lot of known psychological effects revealing that our brain lulls us into believing that we are right no matter what. That everything always is ok with our way of perceiving things and thinking about them, no matter what available evidence says. This could manifest in You believing that there is something special, something wrong with those pictures and tasks shown to You now, and that there is nothing odd about yourself. When the truth is – there is nothing special about these pictures and tasks, but there are many things special about You (us – humans), which is better to be familiar with.
